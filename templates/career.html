ACCELSOBIO

Just as we are rethinking the start-up process for clinical trials, we are rethinking today’s optimal work environment. Great people are the heart of the company, and we believe happy employees produce great results for clients and patients. At Accelsobio, we believe in the following:

Doing Our Part to Save the Planet - Accelsobio employees are all home based. In today’s age of technical know-how, there is no reason for employees to drive into an office, burning fossil fuels and unnecessary electricity.

Diversity - A diverse workforce creates the strongest team, and the clinical trials industry needs more diversity. We pride ourselves on looking for dedicated team members that include all races and nationalities of people. We are LGBTQ+ friendly and appreciate working mothers.

Happy People are Productive, Dedicated Employees - Flexibility is important for managing every day challenges. At Accelsobio, we are open to flexible work schedules, including part-time, 4-day work weeks, and summer’s off. Got to pick your kids up every day from the car rider line or take Fido to the vet? We understand that having a career that allows you to put your family first, is critical to employee satisfaction.

If you are interested in working at Accelsobio, send us your CV and a message detailing what you can bring to the team.
