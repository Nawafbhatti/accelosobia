ACCELSOBIO

Accelsobio provides accelerated solutions for biotechs. We take the lag time out of the startup process by making it our sole focus, connecting sites to studies with responsiveness and dedication.

Services Include:

Site Identification & Feasibility Assessment

Contract and Budget Development & Negotiation

Feasibility Questionnaire Development & Management

Site Qualification Visits

Site Start-up Including (Communication, Document Collection, ICF Management, and Debarment Checks)

Central IRB Selection & Management

Vendor and Start-up Consulting

Our Process

We work with companies of all sizes and in all stages of development. Whether you are looking for a partner to manage your organization’s first study or a team that can improve on previous experiences, we can help.

We’ve seen it all, from teams that have no understanding of study start-up, to teams who are highly experienced, but looking for a solution that can improve their study metrics. Successful project initiation impacts study timelines and budgets in a big way, setting up your study for success. Poor project initiation leads to missed corporate goals, budget and timeline overruns, and patients that don’t have access to novel therapies.

Whether you are looking for solutions for a new study or need to rescue your study from an underperforming vendor, we are your solution. Work on your project begins the same day the contract is signed. Rescue studies can be transitioned from the outgoing vendor in less than 2 weeks, moving your study from despair to progress quickly.

Contact us to discuss the needs of your project and let us help you define a roadmap to success.
